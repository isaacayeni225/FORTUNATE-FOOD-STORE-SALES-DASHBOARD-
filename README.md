# FORTUNATE-FOOD-STORE-SALES-DASHBOARD
## Project Overview: Fortunate Food Store Sales Dashboard  

### Purpose:
The purpose of this Power BI project is to create an interactive dashboard for Fortunate Food Store, a new food store that opened early this year. The dashboard will provide insights into the store's sales performance, enabling data-driven decisions to drive business growth.

### Objective:
To design and develop a comprehensive dashboard that tracks Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and provides an overview of the store's sales trends, product performance, and customer behavior.

### Scope:
The dashboard will utilize sales data collected from the store's first sales period, providing insights into:
- Sales trends and revenue growth
- Top-selling products and categories
- Customer purchasing patterns
- Sales by time of day, day of the week, and month

### Expected Outcome:
The Fortunate Food Store dashboard will enable the owner of the business to:
- Monitor sales performance and identify areas for improvement
- Make informed decisions on inventory management, pricing, and promotions
- Track progress towards business goals and objectives
